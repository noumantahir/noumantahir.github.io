Privacy

Last updated: December 29, 2016

Afrikasa Media is committed to protecting your privacy and developing technology that gives you the most powerful and safe online experience. This Privacy Policy describes the types of information collected from you, what the APP does with it, with whom it shares the information, and how you can correct or change such information. Personally Identifiable Information
This App does not collect personally identifiable information (i.e., information that can be used to identify or contact you, such as your name, email address, or mailing address) from you unless you voluntarily choose to submit that information. If you provide personally identifiable information, that information will be used for the purposes for which you have provided it, and for other purposes that are consistent with this Privacy Policy, and permitted by law. This App does not share personally identifiable information with unrelated third parties except as permitted by law and for all other purposes that are consistent with this Privacy Policy. This App undertakes reasonable steps to protect personally identifiable information you provide.

Use of your Personal Information
Afrikasa Media collects and uses your personal information to operate Afrikasa Media Apps and deliver the services you have requested. Afrikasa Media also uses your personally identifiable information to inform you of other products or services available from Afrikasa Media and its affiliates. Afrikasa Media does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties. Afrikasa Media may, from time to time, contact you on behalf of external business partners about a particular offering that may be of interest to you. In those cases, your unique personally identifiable information (such as your e-mail address, name, address, telephone number) is not transferred to the third party. In addition, Afrikasa Media may share data with trusted partners to help us send you e-mail or postal mail, provide customer support, or arrange for deliveries. All such third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to provide these services to Afrikasa Media, and they are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

Afrikasa Media may keep track of the Apps and pages our customers visit within Afrikasa Media in order to determine what Afrikasa Media services are the most popular. This data is used to deliver customized content and advertising within Afrikasa Media to customers whose behavior indicates that they are interested in a particular subject area. Afrikasa Media Apps will disclose your personal information, without notice, only if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on Afrikasa Media or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of Afrikasa Media; and, (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of Afrikasa Media, or the public.

Security of your Personal Information
Afrikasa Media secures your personal information from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. Afrikasa Media secures the personally identifiable information you provide on computer servers in a controlled, secure environment, protected from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. When personal information (such as a credit card number) is transmitted to other Apps, it is protected through the use of encryption. The SSL is only active when customers log-in or start to register.
